About Beyond1Bar

Welcome to Beyond1Bar, the captivating world of underwater photography! My name is Anto Steele, and I am a passionate scuba diving instructor and photographer based in the Philippines. With my camera in hand, I explore the mesmerizing depths of the ocean to capture the awe-inspiring beauty that lies beneath the surface. Through my lens, I aim to share the enchanting marine life and breathtaking seascapes that fill our oceans. As a diving enthusiast, I have witnessed firsthand the wonders of the underwater world, and I am dedicated to preserving its magic through my photography. Join me on this visual journey as I invite you to dive into the extraordinary and discover the wonders that lie Beyond1Bar.
